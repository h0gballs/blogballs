You can probably find me a bit too easily since I use this username just abot everywhere. Maybe one day I'll come forward and take ownership over this highly-prestigious blog. ## What is the Purpose of Blogballs?
If I'm being honest, I don't really know. Is this a technical blog? A scream-chamber of opinion pieces? A safe-place that isn't some shitty social media for me to share things with friends and family? Another ADHD hyper-fixation that will become irrelevant within 3 months?
